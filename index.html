Introduction

Where to start?

Young. Naive. Unexperienced. How do I manage my finances?

Have you ever wondered how your parents manage their personal finances? Or how could you yourself become an investor? Or thinking simpler, how to manage household incomes?

Have you ever wondered how your parents manage their personal finance?

"I have no special talent, I am just passionately curious" As Albert Einstein said being curious is great. So if you wonder how your parents manage their money talk with them and get informed, it will help you in the future as you can learn from them and also you won´t make the same mistakes.

How could you become an investor?

It is sometimes believed that becoming a young investor specially having a low income its imposible. But it is not! And also the earlier you start to invest, the more money you´ll make. So learn how to be realistic, how to choose where to invest, how to manage your savings and how to pay taxes. What are you waiting for?

How to manage household income?

It is essential to know how to manage our income. Meeting everyday expenses, handling bills and saving can be extremely hard but if you know how to create a buget you will control your money and make the most of it!

About us

We are aiming to tackle this issue by providing a more complete, accessible and attractive education regarding finance to interested individuals that belong to minorities where this inequality has a noticeable impact. This will be done by providing courses where students can be involved and informed on many financial areas. The courses will combine both theoretical and practical teaching, so our students will be able to implement what is learnt into their own circumstances. We will reach out to schools to promote our program, as well as using our social media to reach young audiences. We also have a web where they can find information about us, what we do and what moves us.
